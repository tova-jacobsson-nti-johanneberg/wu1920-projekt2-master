#2 GREYHOUND

Funeral is the thirteenth studio album by American rapper Lil Wayne. It was released on January 31, 2020, by Young Money Entertainment and Republic Records. It features guest appearances by Big Sean, Lil Baby, Adam Levine, Jay Rock, 2 Chainz

#3 CONTAGION

Yeezus is the sixth studio album by American rapper and producer Kanye West. It was released on June 18, 2013, through Def Jam Recordings and Roc-A-Fella Records. West gathered a number of artists and close collaborators for the production.

#4 LITTLE WOMEN

Fine Line is the second studio album by English singer and songwriter Harry Styles, released on 13 December 2019 by Columbia and Erskine Records. Preceded by three singles—"Lights Up", "Watermelon Sugar" and "Adore You".

#5 ABOMINABLE

Cuz I Love You is the third studio album and debut major label LP by American rapper and singer Lizzo. It was released through Nice Life and Atlantic Records on April 19, 2019. Features guest rappers Missy Elliott and Gucci Mane

#6 BIG HERO 6

When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go? är ett musikalbum av Billie Eilish som utgavs i mars 2019. Detta var hennes första riktiga fullängdsalbum. Det utgavs på Interscope Records underetikett Darkroom.
