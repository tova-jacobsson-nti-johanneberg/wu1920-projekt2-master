Fine Line is the second studio album by English singer and songwriter Harry Styles, released on 13 December 2019 by Columbia and Erskine Records. Supported by four singles — "Lights Up", "Watermelon Sugar", "Adore You", and "Falling"[1] — Fine Line debuted at number three on the UK Albums Chart and number one on the Billboard 200, making it Styles' second consecutive number-one album in the US.[2] The album had the third-largest sales week of 2019 in the US and broke the record as the biggest debut from a British male artist since Nielsen SoundScan began.[2] It was the last number one of the 2010s and also the first number one on the Billboard 200 in the 2020s.[3] Fine Line has been described as rock, pop and pop rock, with elements of prog-pop, psychedelic pop, folk, soul, funk and indie pop.
